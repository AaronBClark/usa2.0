  legislative:
    - title: Rebalance incentives away from endless war
      summary: Shift policy toward restraint, accountability, and constitutional process rather than permanent intervention drift.
    - title: Stronger oversight of security commitments
      summary: Increase democratic review of how and why major commitments are made.
  structural:
    - title: Durable constitutional peace constraints
      summary: Consider deeper guardrails that make open-ended war footing harder to normalize.
    - title: Long-range doctrine reset
      summary: Rebuild foreign-policy assumptions around restraint, legitimacy, and sustainable national interest.
