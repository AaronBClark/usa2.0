---
title: Fair Elections
slug: fair-elections
short: More accurate representation, broader competition, and outcomes people can trust.
order: 1
tiers:
  immediate:
    - title: Strengthen election transparency standards
      summary: Improve public visibility into ballot handling, reporting, and administrative processes where existing authority allows.
    - title: Expand access through administrative reform
      summary: Reduce unnecessary friction in registration, access, and voter information through lawful executive and agency action.
  legislative:
    - title: Ranked-choice voting
      summary: Reduce spoiler dynamics and improve majority legitimacy in competitive races.
    - title: Open primaries
      summary: Give more voters a meaningful voice in candidate selection, including independents shut out by closed systems.
  structural:
    - title: Independent redistricting
      summary: Reduce partisan map manipulation and make representation more legitimate over time.
    - title: Durable voting-rights protection
      summary: Establish stronger long-term guarantees that are harder to erode through ordinary political swings.
